Setting Up

Building this blog has been an interesting experience. I wanted something simple, fast, and focused on what matters most: the content.

The design philosophy is minimalist:

  • Clean typography for easy reading
  • Fast loading with no unnecessary bloat
  • Responsive design that works everywhere
  • Focus on the writing itself
